The different features of Windows 7 OS



Learning the different features of Windows OS7 is an important skill to know that you can use in your everyday life. However, Windows OS7 has some many features that a lot of people can find very useful in their everyday life. The video will show you how to get to the features that is most useful such as search and organization, taskbar/start menu, aero-shake, aero-snap, and customization. Since there are many different styles of learning I will go through a few of the different styles. For the visual, audio and physical learners the video will help you understand the material by following alone and by seeing the steps. If you learn better by reading and taking notes then I will have a step by step guide to how you get to the different features and what they do.

How to operate a Virtual Machine



Working in the technology field or just wanting to update your system the Virtual Machine(VM) will be very important in this matter. Using the VM will help you get to know other operating systems and will be able to run more than one at a time. Having a basic knowledge of what it is used for will help you understand how much safer this is than just updating to your actually operating system and risk the fact of it crashing in the long run because you don’t know how it works. In the video it will give you step by step on how to setup up a VM and how to actually put it to use. The video will be recorded on the screen with a voice over so the person that is leaning the lesson will be able to stop and rewind it at any time.

Prepositional Phrase



Prepositions are extremely difficult to master and because there are so few rules for them, the best way is looking them up by reading a lot or memorizing useful phrases. However, Prepositional Phrases are important because they work to connect various parts of a sentence. This video will use a version of “Visual Art Grammar” to teach this topic. First, having the basic lesson will allow a visual and aural component to connect with a large number of learners. Then the Artist will draw “Prepositional Phrase” teacher. The other character and objects will function as the visual demonstration of each sentence. The Prepositional Phrase teacher will come up with a couple of ways to learn the Grammar Basics: Sentence Parts and Sentences, giving the viewers examples of Prepositional Phrases and Arranging Prepositional Phrases. This adds in a movement/ drawings that connects with kinetic learners and a voice over to connect with auditory learners.

Resume

I am making this video to show you the right way to write a resume. It is very important to make sure your resume is done the right way when searching and applying for jobs. A lot of people don’t know the correct way to write a resumes which causes them to miss out on a lot of job opportunities. The video will be going over Formatting, Heading, Skills, Experience, Education, Volunteer Experience, and Award and Honors. The video that I will provide will work well with visual and aural learners.
